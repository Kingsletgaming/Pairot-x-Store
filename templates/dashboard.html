Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate gamers, GameID Vault started as a small Discord community for account trading. Frustrated by the lack of secure platforms for buying and selling gaming accounts, we set out to create a solution.

After numerous successful trades and growing demand, we launched our web platform in 2020 with advanced security features and verification processes. Today, we're proud to be the most trusted marketplace for premium gaming accounts, serving thousands of satisfied customers worldwide.

Our team consists of former professional gamers, cybersecurity experts, and customer service professionals - all united by our love for gaming and commitment to creating the best trading experience.
